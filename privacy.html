This Privacy Policy explains how Esthi ("Esthi," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the Esthi mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

Esthi is designed primarily as a study app. Depending on how you use the app, we may process the following categories of information:

  • Study progress, quiz results, exam history, and app preferences.
  • Language preference and reminder settings.
  • Subscription and purchase status provided by RevenueCat and the app stores.
  • Technical information needed to operate, maintain, and improve the app.

Local Storage

Esthi may store study progress, settings, and app data locally on your device. If you delete the app, locally stored data may also be deleted.

Purchases and Subscriptions

Esthi uses RevenueCat to help manage in-app purchases, subscriptions, entitlements, and purchase restoration. Purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play, depending on your device. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Notifications

If you enable study reminders, Esthi may schedule local notifications on your device. You can disable notifications in the app settings or through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

Esthi may use third-party services to provide app functionality, including RevenueCat for purchase and subscription management. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

How We Use Information

  • To provide study features, progress tracking, and premium access.
  • To restore purchases and manage subscription status.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To maintain app security, reliability, and performance.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of information associated with a support request or other data we may control, contact us at support@esthiapp.com. Some purchase records are managed by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat and may need to be handled through those services.

Children's Privacy

Esthi is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and address the request.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
